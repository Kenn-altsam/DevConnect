About DevConnect KZ

A trusted platform that connects founders with verified IT talent across Kazakhstan.

🎯

Mission

Make hiring in tech faster, safer, and more transparent for startups, businesses, and individual clients.

🧩

Problem

Clients struggle to trust freelancer quality, while talented developers struggle to be noticed without a strong network.

✅

Solution

Verification, structured profiles, clearer expectations, and role-specific pages for clients and developers.

🌍

Vision

Build the default trust layer for Kazakhstan's freelance and startup tech ecosystem.

What makes it different

More than a job board

DevConnect KZ focuses on trust, fit, and real delivery capacity instead of random listings.

Verified entry

2

Clear role pages

Clients and developers each get the right information and path.

3

Local ecosystem focus

Built around Kazakhstan's talent, language needs, and startup culture.
